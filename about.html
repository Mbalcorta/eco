About Us

Our software solutions transform massive datasets into real knowledge.

 

At EcoInteractive, our mission is to provide software solutions that tackle the challenges of managing environmental data and that streamline the management of transportation projects. We supply cost-effective and scalable solutions with industry leading performance. We work side-by-side with our clients to customize our software to fit varied business processes and produce innovative solutions that transform data into integrated knowledge.

Since 1993, our focus has been web-driven software to meet the needs of government agencies and businesses that interact with government agencies. Today our EcoTracker, ProjectTracker and e-GovTracker software solutions allow customers to:

  • Achieve goals early and demonstrate quick success
  • Maintain complete control and ownership of your data
  • Utilize our secure, high-capacity IT infrastructure
  • Pay as you grow
  • Replace current practice of thousands of paper reports stored throughout hundreds of offices
  • Supply decision makers with integrated quality information where it's needed and when it's needed
  • Easily integrate with other government data systems
